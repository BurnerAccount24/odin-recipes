Banana Muffins

Description of the recipe

These banana muffins are moist and delicious. This is an easy recipe for kids to help make and perfect for using up ripe bananas. They're wonderful as-is or with walnuts or chocolate chips.

ingredients

  • Flour
  • Leaveners: Baking soda and baking powder act as leaveners
  • Salt
  • Bananas
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together in one bowl. Mix the wet ingredients in another bowl.
  2. Fold the dry mixture into the wet mixture.
  3. Spoon the batter into prepared muffin tins. Bake the muffins in the preheated oven.
